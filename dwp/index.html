About

DWP is an organization that believes in the importance of freedom and anonymity on the internet. We recognize the value of the internet as a powerful tool for communication and collaboration, but we also acknowledge the potential risks that come with its use. That's why we work tirelessly to promote and protect internet freedom, privacy, and security.

Our mission is to create a world where individuals can express themselves online without fear of censorship, surveillance, or other forms of repression. We believe that the internet should be a space where people can connect, learn, and explore without being tracked or monitored by governments or corporations. We also advocate for the use of encryption and other technologies that can help protect online privacy and security.
